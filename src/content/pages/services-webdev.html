Accessibility Anchored in the Build


We do not bolt accessibility on at the end. We build it into the hull. HTML allows us to structure content in a way that is clear and usable for everyone from screen reader users to keyboard only navigators. We use proper headings, image descriptions and clean markup to make sure your site welcomes every visitor.

An accessible website isn't just good design, it's good business. Accessibility makes your site easier for everyone to use and helps protect you from costly ADA compliance lawsuits. More than that: it's simply the right thing to do. Inclusive design means your website works for all visitors. That's a standard we believe every business should meet.

Clean Code Means Better SEO


Search engines ❤️ HTML! It's simple, fast, and easy for bots to crawl. Your content isn't hidden behind scripts or popups. Secure. Solid. Seaworthy.


HTML websites are more secure than sites that depend on plugins, themes and third party tools. There is no login screen to attack, no database to break and no plugins quietly going out of date behind the scenes.

As platforms become outdated the sites running on them become slower, weaker and more prone to attack. HTML hasn't changed in decades. It is not going out of style and it doesn't have weaknesses to exploit.

Built to Last
